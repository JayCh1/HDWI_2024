HDWI Probabilities

The idea for this page is to create a product that acts similar to QPQF at NOAA's WPC. Each of these figures shows the probability that the forecast HDWI percentile exceeds the chosen threshold for each day in the forecast. (In other words, a 0.8 shading in a 75th percentile image means that 80% of the GEFS members have an HDWI value greater than the 75th percentile for that day.) This product was developed based on a user request. Click on an image to see it full size.
